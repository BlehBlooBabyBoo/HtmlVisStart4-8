Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you deliver school proofs?

Proof galleries are sent 3–5 business days after the shoot. Retakes follow a dedicated makeup day for no extra school interrupt.

Can we order prints for individual athletes?

Yes—parents can purchase direct from the gallery using custom order codes, with print, poster, and digital bundle choices.

Do you support indoor and outdoor gym/lights?

Yes. We bring multiple lighting rigs and moisture-protected gear for indoor gym, field, and stadium environments.
